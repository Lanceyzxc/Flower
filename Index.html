About Us

Marahuyo is a celebration of beauty, craftsmanship, and Filipino artistry. Our name, inspired by an old Tagalog word meaning "to be enchanted", reflects our passion for creating handmade flowers that capture the heart and spark the imagination. Every piece we craft is a labor of love—delicately shaped, carefully colored, and thoughtfully arranged to bring joy that lasts beyond the seasons. Whether it's a gift, a keepsake, or décor for your special moments, our blooms are made to enchant and endure. Founded with a deep appreciation for natural elegance and meaningful details, Marahuyo is more than just a flower shop—it's a story told through petals and passion. We believe in slow, intentional creation, and in supporting local artisans who pour their hearts into every stem and fold. Let yourself be enchanted by flowers that never fade. Welcome to Marahuyo.
